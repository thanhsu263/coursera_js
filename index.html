FPT University Ho Chi Minh

About

FPT University Ho Chi Minh is one of Vietnam's leading private universities, featuring a modern educational model focused on information technology, business, and fields related to digital transformation. The university offers a dynamic learning environment with strong industry connections and high employment rates.
